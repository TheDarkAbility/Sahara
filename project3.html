Project Overview

In 2022, I collaborated on a data project with La Manivelle, an association in Geneva that promotes the circular economy through object sharing. My key contributions included:

  • Designing and implementing data collection methods to assess membership growth potential
  • Analyzing existing initiatives and proposing strategies to increase membership and enhance object sharing

This project strengthened my skills in data analysis and strategic planning, while expanding my network within the circular economy sector in Geneva.
