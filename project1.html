Project Overview

This Master's Thesis explores the complex interactions between tourism development and environmental and social concerns in Fiji, focusing on Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) and public participation in decision-making.

The research was validated by the University of Geneva and furthered my commitment to sustainability research.
