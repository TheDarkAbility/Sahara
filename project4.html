Project Overview

In 2020, I organized and led a humanitarian aid initiative to support coastal and rural villages in Fiji affected by the COVID-19 pandemic and Cyclone Yasa. My efforts included:

  • Raising $10,000 USD through an online fundraising campaign
  • Coordinating the delivery of essential items to over 180 households
  • Personally overseeing distribution efforts, demonstrating leadership and project management skills

This project underscored my commitment to humanitarian aid and community support, particularly in times of crisis.
